Our Story

A Place of Learning
and Belonging

Central Christian Academy is a community dedicated to holistic education — where every student is known by name, inspired by purpose, and equipped for life beyond the classroom.

We blend rigorous academics with faith-based formation to raise the next generation of thoughtful, courageous, and compassionate individuals ready to make a difference.

Our faculty are not simply teachers — they are mentors, guides, and fellow seekers of truth, committed to the flourishing of each unique child in their care.

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire — and we light that fire with the love of Christ."

— Our Mission Statement
